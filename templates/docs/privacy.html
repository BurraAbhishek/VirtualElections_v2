Privacy Policy

As a player in the digital economy, we are concerned with the protection of the personal data of our users. We implement a process of continuous improvement of our compliance with the General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR).

Who is responsible for processing your personal data?

Within the meaning of the GDPR, the controller is the entity that defines for what purpose(s) and how your personal data is used.

The data controller is the organization which conducts the elections on this site. This website solves the problem of conducting distance elections without advertisements. Use of this site is also completely free.

As a requirement in the election process, we may collect and process the personal data relating to our users. These different data processing operations are documented in this Privacy Policy.

What data is collected about you and for what reasons?

The following table describes our data collection policies in general:

Data collected Purpose Justification of the purpose Maximum data retention period
Information related to your browser and your terminal (HTTP(S) request) Connection to this website; detection of bugs and attempted cyber attacks Legitimate interest of the website (article 6.1.f of the GDPR) 1 year following data collection, then legally applicable limitation period.
Session Cookie Improved browsing experience for registered users Legitimate interest of the website (article 6.1.f of the GDPR) The duration of the browsing session, subject to the configuration of these cookies by the user
CSRF Token Cookie Prevention of Cross-Site Request Forgery Legitimate interest of the website (article 6.1.f of the GDPR) The duration of the browsing session, subject to the configuration of these cookies by the user

While registering as a voter, the following table describes our data collection policies:

Voter name Identification of the voter. Consent (article 6.1.a of the GDPR) Until withdrawal of consent, then legally applicable limitation period.
Voter date of birth Enforcing age restrictions of the voter Required while conducting elections Legally applicable retention period.

Who has access to your data?

Only you and the site administrators have access to the data.
The administrators can access your data only for the purpose of the smooth conduct of the elections.
This website never serves advertisements, and never tracks you across the web.
